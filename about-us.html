About Sithara

Sithara Krishnakumar

THE SINGING STAR

Sithara is an acclaimed Playback singer and a known Ghazal singer. A graded artiste of All India Radio, she is making her presence felt in the music industry following a string of successes in musical talent shows. She has rendered her voice to over 300 film songs in Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu and Kannada languages. A singer who respects and takes her profession seriously, she likes to learn and experiment with folk and fusion genres of music. Moreover, Sithara is a trained classical dancer.

1 July 1986 | Humble Beginnings

Born in Malappuram to K. M. Krishnakumar, an academician and Saly Krishnakumar, Sithara showed a keen interest in music from a very early age. She acheived her first award as the "Best Female Singer" at the "Baburaj Memorial Awards" in 2002. She has won several awards ever since.

Chasing Dreams

She started off her art life as a dancer and eventually became a playback singer. She was trained in Carnatic Music by Sri Ramanattukara Satheesan Master and Palai CK Ramachandran. Sithara also received extensive education in Hindustani Classical music from Ustad Fiyaz Khan. She is also a classical dancer trained by Kalamandalam Vinodini.

Project Malabaricus

Project Malabaricus is a musical band started in September 2017 lead by Sithara Krishnakumar which features contemporarised folk and classical songs. They make their own new songs and re-generate old songs in their own style. From the starting to till now they have performed on so many stages in front of different varieties of audiences. In addition to the stage performances, Project Malabaricus performs their songs in Music Mojo in Kappa TV. Project Malabaricus brought in to the idea of 'Thodi', a garden of tunes that blooms into your hearts. 'Thodi' comprises with snippets of songs that we nurture in to the nature.

EDAM

EDAM (Embrace Dance And Music) is a music & Dance school started by Sithara Krishnakumar in partnership with her friends on 8th October 2019. At EDAM they nurtures our creativity so that your art can mirror your individuality. Training for classical music, classical dance and instrumental music is available at EDAM. As the name implies EDAM is a 'space' that nurtures your talents.
